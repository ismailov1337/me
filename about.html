My story

From the moment I wrote my first "Hello, World" program, I knew I wanted to build things that matter. Growing up in Uzbekistan, I didn't always have access to advanced CS resources — but that challenge fueled my determination to learn on my own.

Through online platforms, open-source projects, and countless hours of trial and error, I taught myself Java, Python, and C++, learning not just how to code — but how to think logically and creatively.

Technology is most powerful when it empowers people. — Something I learned from teaching

I've become deeply interested in cybersecurity — the invisible shield that protects everything technology enables. My goal is to contribute to research in cybersecurity, open-source development, and help bridge the gap between technology and education in developing regions.
